About Boba Bear

At BobaBear, we believe that every sip should bring joy, warmth, and a little bit of magic to your day. Born from a passion for creativity and quality, our mission is to offer more than just a drink – we’re serving moments of happiness in a cup. With a blend of traditional techniques and a sprinkle of fun, each cup of BobaBear bubble tea is made with love, using only the finest ingredients and the freshest fruits.

Founded in the heart of New York City in 2015, BobaBear has quickly become a beloved brand, with over 100 locations across the country. We’re not just about great tea – we’re about creating experiences that make you smile, share laughter, and make memories with friends and family.

Our dream? To bring the world together, one delicious bubble at a time, and to inspire everyone to embrace their playful side while savoring the perfect bubble tea. Whether you’re in the mood for a classic milk tea or an adventurous fruity creation, BobaBear is here to make your day a little sweeter and a lot more fun.

In 2019, we launched National Boba Day, a celebration of all things boba, to honor the global community of bubble tea lovers who live and breathe the joy of every chewy bite and refreshing sip.

At BobaBear, we’re not just making tea – we’re creating smiles, one bubble at a time.
